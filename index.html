Who We Are

Spotvin is a dynamic, boutique design agency located in historic Walkerville in Windsor, Ontario.

At it’s heart, Spotvin’s creativity drives everything we do. The cookie cutter approach has become the norm for advertising and marketing agencies, but we have always believed that strong results come from great ideas. Deep down we’re design nerds, sweating the details isn’t just our job - it’s our passion.

Spotvin’s team works hard, produces great results, and we have fun while we’re at it.

Why Spotvin?

While we pride ourselves on our creativity and the great ideas we bring to every campaign, you’ll quickly see we’re not lacking in other areas.

Strategy is front of mind in all we do, ensuring that our work not only looks great, but is also results-driven, with strong emphasis on communication with you at every phase of your project.

Our clients deserve the most thoughtful, creative and successful campaigns. Above all else, we deliver.

What do we do? Passionately and expertly “craft creative”.
